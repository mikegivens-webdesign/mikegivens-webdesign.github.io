5 Great Ways to Increase Social Media Engagement

1. Comment on others posts
Engaging with similar brands on social media can boost your visibility by tapping into overlapping audiences, increasing the likelihood of shares, mentions, and collaborations. It signals relevance within your niche, encourages algorithmic favor by generating interaction, and helps build community credibility and trust through association.

2. Post on a daily basis
Research shows that posting on a daily basis increases how often your content is suggested in algorithms. Pages who post daily have more engagement and see more growth over time.

3. Reply to comments on your posts.
Not only do replies draw attention to your business, but they create a sense of community with the viewers, who are likely to come back. Additionally replying and liking comments increases your viewership by showing your profile to followers of the commenter.

4. Interactive content
On many platforms, such as instagram, it is possible to allow viewers to vote, or share their opinions. These posts increase viewer engagement by making them feel like part of a community.

5. Video shorts
Video shorts are the most popular form of media consumption right now. This means that if you are making short videos, on Tiktok, Youtube, Facebook, or all, you are a step ahead of other businesses in exposure, especially if you make videos concerning your local area.
